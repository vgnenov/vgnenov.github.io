FEATURED PROJECT

Chicago Graffiti Removal Tracking Tool

Project Purpose

Each day the City of Chicago receives graffiti removal requests. These requests are input and tracked through their 311 reporting system. This project explores the data from the 311 Service Requests and will observe trends as well as tell a story through data visualization.

Process and Results

Utilizing Jupyter Lab to clean the data that was collected from the City of Chicago database my team and I then turned to SQL and Leaftlet, D3.js and HTML/CSS to visualize the data and spot trends and which areas had the biggest graffiti problems. The above line plot outlines the trend that majority of reported graffiti grievences were issued to the city at the beginning of the week, typically on a Monday or Tuesday. Using the Leaflet library, we were able to plot each 311 requests for graffiti removal and visualize the outcomes in a cluster and a heatmap.
